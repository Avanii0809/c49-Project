Here are the steps to make an origami heart:

  • Step 1) Take an origami paper, if not then you can use a square shaped paper.
  • Step 2) Fold the bottom corner up to the top corner and crease well.
  • Step 3) Take the right points over to the left and make a small crease along the bottom edge to mark the middle.
  • Step 4) Unfold the previous step.
  • Step 5) Take the right point and bring it up and to the right at an angle away from the top point.
  • Step 6) Do the same thing on the left side, try to make it match the right side.
  • Step 7) Fold the bottom point up a little, to form the cat's chin.
  • Step 8) Flip the cat over to the other side.
  • Step 9) Fold the top points down, until it reaches to about where the left and right points would line up.
  • Step 10) You can fold just one layer behind to have a two coloured cat, or fold both of the layers behind.
  • Step 11) Flip the cat over to the other side and fold the left and right points in a little to round out the face.

    • Voila! Your origami heart is ready.

  • Step 12) (Optional) You can now draw a face for your cat.
