Here are the steps to make an origami heart:

  • Step 1) Take an origami paper, if not then you can use a square shaped paper.
  • Step 2) Fold the right edge of the paper over to the left and unfold it
  • Step 3) Fold the bottom edge of the paper up to the top edge and unfold it.
  • Step 4) Fold the bottom edge of the paper up to the central crease.
  • Step 5) Flip the paper over to the other side.
  • Step 6) Fold the bottom right corner diagonally up to the left (make sure it aligns with the vertical crease).
  • Step 7) Fold the bottom left corner the same way (aligning with the vertical crease).
  • Step 8) Flip the paper over to the other side.
  • Step 9) Fold the left and right edges to meet in the middle (use the help of the verticle crease).
  • Step 10) Fold the top left and right corners diagonally inwards.
  • Step 11) Bring the top point down to the bottom point and start to flatten it down.
  • Step 12) Insert the top point inside the lower pocket.
  • Step 13) Flatten the top section of the heart.
  • Step 14) Fold the top left and right corners diagonally inwards.
  • Step 15) Fold the two top points of the heart down a little.
  • Step 16) Flip the paper over.

Voila! Your origami heart is ready.
